Practice Areas

Supreme Court

Delhi High Court

AAC represents its clients before the Delhi High Court in the cases pertaining to service & employment laws, labour & industrial laws, paramilitary forces employment disputes, property laws, matrimonial disputes, civil disputes and criminal trials.

Learn More

Central Administrative Tribunal

AAC represents its clients before the Central Administrative Tribunal, Principal Bench, New Delhi, in the cases pertaining to service and employment laws. The Central Administrative Tribunal was established under Article 323-A of the Constitution of India for adjudication of disputes with respect to recruitment and conditions of service of persons appointed to public services and posts in connection with the affairs of the Union or other authorities under the control of the Government.

Learn More

Delhi School Tribunal

AAC represents its clients before the Delhi School Tribunal, New Delhi, in the cases pertaining to the Delhi School Education Act, 1973. The Delhi School Tribunal, New Delhi was constituted under the Delhi School Education Act, 1973 and adjudicates the employment related disputes of the employees of the private schools (aided as well as unaided) in Delhi. Subject-wise jurisdiction of the Tribunal is confined to dismissal, removal or reduced in rank of the school employees.

Learn More

Labour Courts & Industrial Tribunals

AAC represents its clients before the Labour Courts and Industrial Tribunals in the cases pertaining to The Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, The Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, The Contract Labour (Regulation & Abolition) Act, 1970, etc.

Learn More

District Courts and Consumer Forums

AAC represents its clients before the all the District Courts and Consumer Forums in the cases pertaining to property disputes, consumer disputes, matrimonial disputes and criminal trials.
